Christopher Brobbey is an innovator from Pomadze, Central Region, is tackling the high cost of animal feed, the lack of a convenient market for farmers, and the country's reliance on imported meat products.

Solution To Problem

His solution involves creating a simple marketplace where farmers can sell their animals directly to trusted consumers. Additionally, he aims to add value to locally produced meat by smoking, freezing, or processing it into sausages.

Support

To further support farmers, he plans to develop organic animal feed, reducing costs and enabling more sustainable livestock farming. He seeks funding and support to expand his impact.
